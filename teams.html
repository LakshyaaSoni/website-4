Teams

The race will consist of 6 teams. Each team also has a captain, who is in charge of managing their team's run schedule and who will be the main point-of-contact for the team. Captains are indicated below with a star. If you are a team captain, you should have received a message from the organizers with more information.

These assignments were created such that the estimated total time for each team is as close as possible. Our hope is that this results in a more interesting race both for the runners and those watching the race.
