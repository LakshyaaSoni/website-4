Captain Duties

  1. Point of contact before and during the race.
    • Help answer questions for your team and help them out and cheer them on during the race.
  2. Work with your team to create a team name.
    • It does not have to relate to your team’s assigned color.
    • It can be whatever your team wants, but it does have to be within reason.
    • Please make a post in the captain’s room in the discord whenever your team decides upon a name.
    • This is due by May 1st.
  3. Encourage the team to hang out in voice chat if they’d like.
    • If the current runner is not in voice chat, then encourage your team to hang out in the runner’s twitch chat.
    • Be as supportive as possible for any runners of your team who are running late at night.
  4. Schedule runs for your team.
    • Make sure everyone is available for their run.
    • You’ll want to make a typed out schedule either on some website, a google document, or Horaro.
    • Please post your schedule in the captain’s room whenever it is made.
    • This is due by July 10th.
  5. Make sure runners are ready to start their run when it starts.
    • Try to have them ready to go at least an hour before they’re scheduled to start just in case.
  6. If someone drops unexpectedly before the race, make a post in the captain’s room and get in contact with an organizer immediately about it.
    • If someone drops unexpectedly during the race, make a post in the #emergency-help channel and we will help address the situation immediately.
  7. Leave someone in charge of your duties while you sleep during the marathon.
    • You can have an assistant captain if you want someone to take over your duties while you’re not there.
  8. Team channel setup.
    • You have the option to have your team stream to a single channel, rather than individual channels.
    • You would be in charge of adding info panels, managing the stream key, etc. You can also add channel artwork, layouts and other information.
    • The way you handle your stream is up to you and your team. You can name your team’s twitch channel however you want to.
    • Please post your team channel in the captain’s room in the discord whenever you make it.
    • If your team does not want to have a team channel, you don’t have to have one.
    • If your team is choosing to not make a team channel, please make a post in the captain’s room saying so.
    • Your team’s decision is due by June 1st.
  9. Mitigate conflicts between team members.
    • If there is a conflict between you and a member of your team, bring it to an organizer.
