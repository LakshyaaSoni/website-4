Signup!

Use the form below to sign up for the 1545! Select your games from the column on the right, then add your PB and estimate for each one.

Note on estimates: The 1545 is not like a normal speedrunning marathon. We aren't interested in your worst-case estimate. The best way to get a good estimate for us is to do 3-5 runs of your game and take the average time you get. While this may not be entirely accurate, it gives us a good idea of consistency relative to your PB.

Use the handles on the game cards to order them in the order of your willingness to run them. Please only submit games that you are willing to run. We will try our best to assign you to your prioritized games, but we can't make any guarantees for that.
